Foccacia Bread

Easy Bread Recipe

Shockingly Easy No-Knead Focaccia

Ingredients

  • 1 ¼-oz. envelope active dry yeast (about 2¼ tsp.)
  • 2 tsp. honey
  • 5 cups (625 g) all-purpose flour
  • 5 tsp. Diamond Crystal or 1 Tbsp. Morton kosher salt
  • 6 Tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil, divided, plus more for hands
  • 4 Tbsp. unsalted butter, plus more for pan
  • Flaky sea salt
  • 2-4 garlic cloves

Steps

  1. Mix lukewarm water, sugar and the yeast in a bowl.
  2. Add the flour and mix to get a homogenous dough.
  3. Let it rise at room temperature for twenty four hours.
  4. Oil a baking sheet and place your dough and rest for another hour.
  5. Bake it at 200°C for 30-45 minutes.

Cook's note

It goes really well with confit garlic after baked.
